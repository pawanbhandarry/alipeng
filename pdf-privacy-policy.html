Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the PDF Reader & Editor App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

The App is a full-featured PDF reader and editor that allows users to view, edit, rename PDF files, and convert images from the device gallery into PDF documents.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Files and Documents

The App allows users to:

  • Open and read PDF files
  • Edit PDF documents
  • Rename PDF files
  • Convert images from the device gallery into PDF files

All files accessed or created by the App remain on the user’s device. We do not upload or store documents on external servers unless the user explicitly chooses to share them.

1.2 Gallery Access

The App may request permission to access your photo gallery solely to allow image-to-PDF conversion. Only images selected by the user are accessed.

1.3 Device & Usage Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

This data is used only to improve app stability and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide PDF viewing, editing, and conversion features
  • Allow users to manage and rename PDF files
  • Improve performance and fix bugs
  • Ensure proper functionality and security

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing.

3. Data Storage and Security

All file processing occurs locally on the user’s device. We do not automatically upload or back up user files. Any sharing is fully initiated by the user.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data. Data may only be disclosed if required by law or to protect the security of the App and its users.

5. App Permissions

  • Storage / Files: To read, edit, rename, and save PDF files
  • Photos / Media: To select images for Image-to-PDF conversion

6. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
