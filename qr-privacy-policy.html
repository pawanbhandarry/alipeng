Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the QR Scanner App ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

The App allows users to scan QR codes and barcodes using their device camera.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Camera Access

The App requires access to your device camera to scan QR codes and barcodes. Camera data is processed in real time and is not stored or recorded.

1.2 Scanned Content

QR code content is displayed only to the user. We do not collect, store, or transmit scanned data to external servers.

1.3 Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

This information is used solely to improve app performance and stability.

2. How We Use Information

  • Enable QR and barcode scanning
  • Display scanned results to the user
  • Improve app reliability and performance

We do not use data for advertising, tracking, or marketing.

3. Data Storage

The App does not store scanned data by default. Any saving or sharing of scanned content is fully controlled by the user.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
